Tacos Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States(US)?

Tacos likely appeared in the U.S. in the early 20th century, gaining popularity through Mexican communities and establishments like Alejandro Morales' taco truck in 1920s Los Angeles. They became widely popular nationwide in the 1960s and 1970s, especially with the rise of Taco Bell.
